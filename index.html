Stanford University is seeking mothers and infants 6 months of age or younger for a study of how infants develop.

This study is conducted in the Department of Psychology at Stanford University. We are currently assessing mother-infant pairs in order to learn more about families and how infants develop. Our work may help to improve prevention and early intervention options of emotional disorders.

Your participation in this study could help us reach our goal of preventing emotional disorders in children.


To be eligible you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a child under 6 months
  • Be able to read and speak English
  • Have no immediate plans to leave the Bay Area

Eligible participants will receive:

  • $25 per hour
  • A toy for your baby
  • BABIES Junior Scientist t-shirt



  • A picture of your child's brain
  • Certificate of completion for your records or baby book
