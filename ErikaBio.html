Erika Puente

Research Assistant

I joined the Gotlib lab as a research assistant in October '16. On scan team, I assist with MRI scans and help process MRI data. I am getting more involved with interacting with mothers during the BABIES scanning sessions. I also helped design this website (first built by Josie Furbershaw). I graduated from Harvard University in May 2016 with a B.A. in Psychology and hope to pursue a clinical psychology PhD. My longer-term aspirations revolve around the idea that a deeper understanding of the brain, behavior, and genes can have a meaningful impact in patient care.
